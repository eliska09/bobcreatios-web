About Me

Since i was a small kid, i have always been interested to computer-related things, in 2020 i learnt the basics of roblox studio, and in 2021 i learnt scripting.
Since that i have been making games for the people.
In 2023 i had a idea to make my own game making company.
I came up with the name Bob Creations, because of my roblox display name.
Since 2023 i have been making games to the public and one day i hope to be a full-time developer.

Thank you for your support! It means a lot
